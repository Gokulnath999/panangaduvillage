A Land of Tradition & Peace

பனங்காடு (Panangadu) என்பது வேலூர் மாவட்டம் உட்பட்ட ஒரு சிறிய மற்றும் அமைதியான கிராமம். இது அணைக்கட்டு (Anaicut) வட்டத்தில் அமைந்துள்ளது. இயற்கை சூழலும், பசுமையும் நிறைந்த ஒரு பாரம்பரிய கிராமமாகும்.

With a population of around 150 families, PANANGADU thrives on agriculture, handicrafts, and community spirit. Every sunrise brings the scent of jasmine from the temple courtyard, the sound of cowbells in the morning mist, and the sight of women in vibrant saris drawing water from the village well.

Panangadu கிராமத்தில் முக்கிய திருவிழாக்கள்:
  • பொங்கல்
  • தீபாவளி
  • கோவில் திருவிழா (தேர் திருவிழா)
  • ஆடி திருவிழா

இவை கிராம ஒற்றுமையை அதிகரிக்கும்.

Our Living Culture

A tapestry woven from centuries of tradition, faith, and celebration.

🪔

Festivals

From the luminous splendor of Diwali to the devotion of Porkodi Amman Thiruvizha, our calendar is filled with celebrations that bring every family together under the open sky.

  • Diwali – Festival of Lights
  • Pongal – Harvest Thanksgiving
  • Porkodi Amman Thiruvizha
  • Vedappan Thiruvizha
  • Ramar Marriage
  • Ramar Bajanai
  • Vinnayagar Chadurthi
  • Navratri – Nine Nights of Dance
🍲

Village Cuisine

Our food is as rich as our soil — cooked in clay pots over wood fires, seasoned with home-grown spices, and served with the warmth of a mother's love.

  • Dal-Bati — village lentil bread
  • Rice & Sambar on banana leaf
  • Freshly churned butter & chai
  • Seasonal vegetables from the farm
🌾

Farming Traditions

Agriculture is not just our livelihood — it is our identity. Generations of farmers have tended the same land with the same devotion, reading the skies and seasons like an open book.

  • Rice, wheat & sugarcane cultivation
  • Bullock-cart & modern ploughing
  • Organic & natural farming
  • Community harvest celebrations

"They preserve culture, traditions, and natural living."

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reach Panangadu?

You can reach us via Vellore by local bus or taxi. We are located in the Anaicut Taluk.

What are the major festivals celebrated?

We celebrate Porkodiamman Thiruvizha and other major Tamil festivals like Pongal and Diwali.

Are visitors allowed in the village?

Yes, visitors are most welcome to experience our culture and heritage.
